# AI Decision Intelligence Assistant – Project Scoping

## 1. Business Context

A €40M ARR B2B SaaS company operating in Europe and APAC is experiencing slowing growth driven by increasing churn and lower expansion rates.

The board has requested a clear, data-driven 12‑month growth plan under constrained resources.

## 2. Strategic Problem

The company must decide where to allocate limited growth investments to maximize ARR growth while controlling churn.

## 3. Key Decisions to Make

1.Should the company prioritize acquisition or expansion?
2.Which customer segments should be deprioritized despite generating revenue?
3.Should sales capacity be reallocated toward larger accounts or renewal-focused teams?

## 4. Constraints & Assumptions

1.Growth budget capped at +10%
2.Sales & Customer Success headcount unchanged
3.Usage_score is a proxy for real product usage
4.Dataset is static (no causal inference)

## 5. Target Users

1.Head of Strategy
2.VP Sales
3.CFO
4.Consulting team

## 6. Value Proposition

1.Rapid strategic diagnosis (minutes, not weeks)
2.Clear prioritization of growth levers
3.Decision-ready recommendations under constraints
4.Executive-level synthesis

## 7. Out of Scope

-Individual churn prediction
-Real-time system
-CRM integration
